# Pantheon

## Register
product

## Product purpose
A public dashboard for Pantheon: a multi-agent, on-chain hedge fund on the Mantle Sepolia testnet. Three autonomous AI agents (Hermes, Pythia, Demeter) propose trades on a fixed cycle; an allocator scores them and moves real testnet USDC through an on-chain vault; PnL settles back on-chain. The dashboard's job is to let a visitor *watch the agents reason and trade live*, judge their track record, and deposit testnet USDC to back them. The reasoning feed is the centerpiece, not the metrics.

## Users
Crypto-native visitors and hackathon judges evaluating whether autonomous agents can be trusted with capital. They are fluent in trading terminals and DeFi apps. They arrive skeptical and want evidence: real on-chain settlement, legible agent reasoning, an honest track record. Secondary: first-time wallet users who need a guided path to deposit.

## Brand & tone
Named for the Greek titaness of divine order and fair judgment. Tone: composed, exact, trustworthy, a little severe. Not playful, not hypey, not "crypto bro." The interface should feel like a serious instrument that happens to be watching machines think, closer to a quant desk or a control room than a consumer fintech app.

## Anti-references
- AI-generated SaaS slop: glassmorphism, glowing gradient blobs, gradient buttons/bars, big-hero-number + identical stat-card grid.
- Crypto neon-on-black. Terminal green-on-black retro.
- Over-rounded, over-animated consumer fintech (Robinhood-style confetti).

## Strategic principles
- Evidence over decoration. Every pixel should reinforce "this is real and you can verify it."
- The live reasoning feed is the protagonist; portfolio metrics are a calm supporting readout.
- Honest framing: testnet, unaudited, capped. Never hide the disclaimers, never dramatize them.
- Restraint signals competence. Density is fine for this audience.
